About

My journey into Web Development started in 2018 with earning a Grow with Google Scholarship that allowed me to take the Udacity Front End Web Development course which I completed in October of 2018 with a Nanodegree.
Born and raised in Germany, I graduated from Bonn University in 2010 (Master's degree in Cultural Anthropology). For several years I have worked as conference manager at an international company, organizing events in many countries around the world.
After relocating to the Mid-West in 2013 I have tought German, organized a group tour to Germany, and finally worked as a Digital Marketing Coordinator for a non-profit organization.
Currently I am working on enhancing my skills in Web Development and will update this website frequently with all the new and shiny projects I create.
